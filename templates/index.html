Title: Predicting the Default of Credit Cards holders

Author: Ayan Chowdhury

Problem Statement: Financial threats are displaying a trend about the credit risk of commercial banks as the incredible improvement in the financial industry has arisen. In this way, one of the biggest threats faces by commercial banks is the risk prediction of credit clients. The goal is to predict the probability of credit default based on credit card owner's characteristics and payment history.

Domain: Banking

Technology: Machine Learning
